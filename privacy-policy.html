Effective Date: July 12, 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how I, SmileLulz ("I", "me", or "my"), collect, use, and protect your information when you use my mobile games and services.

1. Ads and Third-Party Services

My mobile games may display ads provided by third-party networks such as Google AdMob. These networks may collect and use certain information (such as device information or user behavior) to show personalized ads, in accordance with their own privacy policies.

For more information, please review Google's Privacy Policy and AdMob's data practices.

2. In-App Purchases

Some of my mobile games offer in-app purchases (IAPs). These transactions are processed by the respective app stores (such as Google Play), and I do not collect or store any personal payment information.

3. Google Play Games Services

My games may integrate Google Play Games Services to offer features such as achievements, cloud saves, or leaderboards. These services are governed by Google's own privacy practices. I do not directly collect any personal data through these features.

4. Data Collection

At this time, I do not collect any personal information directly from users. However, third-party services (such as AdMob or Google Play Games Services) may collect data as described above.

In the future, I may introduce features that involve limited data collection (e.g., multiplayer, cloud save, analytics). If that happens, this Privacy Policy will be updated accordingly.

5. Children's Privacy

My games are designed to be suitable for all ages, including children under 13. I do not knowingly collect any personal information from children. If you believe that I have unintentionally collected such information, please contact me, and I will promptly remove it.

6. Future Updates

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time to reflect changes in features, services, or applicable laws. I encourage you to review it periodically.
